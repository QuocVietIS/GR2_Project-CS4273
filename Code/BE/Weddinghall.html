Natural Concept

Our customers will enjoy a mouthwatering menu with quality guaranteed. As a group we use only the very best natural ingredients, and it’s our mission to deliver exceptional service, on every visit.

All the beef served in our restaurants is 100% grazed and finished on natural grasses and is certified ‘Never Ever’. This guarantees they have ‘Never Ever’ been given Antibiotics, Hormones, Growth promoters or fed grain.

The result is a healthy product with a natural taste, which is high in omega 3, Vitamin E, Conjugated Linoleic Acid (CLA’s), is high in nutrients and low in saturated fats.
